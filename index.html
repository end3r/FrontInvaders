onSpaceStart

Simple game based on the Mibbu microframework,
made to promote onGameStart - first HTML5 game conference.

onGameStart will take place in Warsaw, Poland on 22-23 september 2011.

Info

Take control of the rocket and fly as high as You can.
Watch out for the balloons, birds, airplanes, meteors and satellites.

Controls

Use arrow keys to move your rocket. Hit spacebar to pause the game.
